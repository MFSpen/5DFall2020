BANKSY @ NEU?

Banksy, one of the worlds premeire street artists, appears to have left his mark on a set of stairs close to Matthews arena at Northeastern Universities Boston campus. Banksy is famous for expressing his, as described by Rosita Auriemma, provocative and shocking takes on aspects of society. He is only known by the name Banksy, and appears to have kept his identity a complete secret despite his fame.
